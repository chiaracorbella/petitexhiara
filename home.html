chiara is a queer multidisciplinary creative who thrives at the intersection of visual arts, language and human connection. she's a world traveler, an educator and a translator. for her, art is a way to tell stories and connect with her overwhelming emotions - a tool to understand herself and others.

chiara's artistic journey began with fiber arts, continuing through illustration and graphic design, and most recently landing in her newfound love for ceramics. though born and raised in milan, italy, her experience and aesthetics were deeply shaped by the time she spent in amsterdam and los angeles, where she truly found her creative voice.

in her work, she likes playing with shapes and colors, embracing childlike expression, imperfection, and often experimenting with new styles and media. when she's not creating, you'll find her taking care of her plants, yearning about the past, rollerskating around town, or hunting for zines from fellow independent artists.

she's currently based in berlin, germany.
